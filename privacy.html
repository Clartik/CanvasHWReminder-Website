Privacy Policy for Canvas HW Reminder

Last Updated: November 10, 2024

Canvas HW Reminder (the "App") respects your privacy and is committed to being transparent about its data practices. This privacy policy outlines how the App collects, uses, and stores your information.

1. Information Collection and Usage

The App only collects the following information, which is stored solely on your device:

  • Canvas API Access Token: Required to authenticate your account and retrieve your Canvas data. This token is stored locally on your device and is not accessible to anyone, including the developer.
  • Base Institution URL: Needed to connect to your institution's Canvas instance. This information is stored locally on your device.
  • User Settings: Any preferences or settings configured in the App are saved locally on your device for a personalized experience.

    • 2. Data Storage and Security

    All information collected by the App is stored locally on your device. The App does not transmit, share, or store any data externally. This means that the developer has no access to your information, and the data remains private to you.

    3. Third-Party Services

    The App does not use any third-party services or analytics platforms that collect user data.

    4. Changes to This Privacy Policy

    Any updates to this privacy policy will be reflected in the App and on the website. We encourage you to periodically review this policy to stay informed about our data practices.

    5. Contact Information
