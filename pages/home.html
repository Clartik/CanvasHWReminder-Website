What Does It Do?

Canvas HW Reminder automatically retrieves your HW from Canvas every hour and reminds you when you want to be reminded before the due date.

Customize to Your Liking

When To Remind You?

Choose how in advance you want to be reminded about your HW deadlines.

How Long Past Due Should An Assignment Stay Visible?

Choose how long you want an assignment that you missed from disappearing from your course cards.

Don't Need Reminders for Some Assignments?

Right click on any assignment to remove it from the reminders list.
Right click again to add it back to the reminders list.

Show Exact Due Dates

Choose whether you want to see exactly when an assignment is due rather than a relative due date from your time.

Always Expand All Course Cards

Choose whether you want all course cards to expand automatically regardless of any assignments are due or not.

Keep Notification Reminders On Screen

Choose whether you want the notification reminders on screen until dismissed or not.

Silence Notifications

Choose whether you want silent notifications or not.

Launch on System Bootup

Choose whether you want Canvas HW Reminder to boot up alongside your computer or not.
(It is highly recommended to leave this enabled to stay reminded about your HW)

Minimize on App Launch

Choose whether you want Canvas HW Reminder to minimize to system tray as the app bootups or not.

Minimize on App Close

Choose whether you want Canvas HW Reminder to minimize to system tray when closed or not.
